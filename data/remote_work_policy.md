
Remote Work Policy
Eligibility
Employees may be eligible for a hybrid remote work schedule after completing a 6-month probationary period. Fully remote positions are available only for specific roles and require approval from department heads.
Equipment
The company will provide essential equipment, including a laptop and monitor. Employees are responsible for ensuring they have a reliable and secure internet connection. A stipend of $50 per month is provided to cover internet costs.
Core Hours
All remote employees are expected to be available online and responsive during the core business hours of 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time.

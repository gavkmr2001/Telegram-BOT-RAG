# Technology FAQ

## How do I reset my VPN password?
To reset your VPN password, please visit the internal IT portal at `it.portal.internal` and navigate to the "Password Reset" section. You will need to answer your security questions.

## How do I request new software?
All software requests must be submitted via a ticket on the IT Helpdesk system. Please include a business justification for the software. Requests are typically reviewed within 48 hours.

## My laptop is slow. What should I do?
First, please try restarting your machine. If the issue persists, run the "System Health Check" application available on your desktop. If problems continue, please raise a ticket with the IT Helpdesk and include the logs from the health check.
